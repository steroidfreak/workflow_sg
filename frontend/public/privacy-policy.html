Privacy Policy

Effective date: 24 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how Workflow SG ("Workflow SG," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and protects your information when you visit www.workflow.sg, engage with our web applications, or interact with our services. By using our site or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account details: Name, company, email address, and authentication credentials you provide when creating an account or requesting access.
  • Communications: Messages you send through chat, contact forms, or email, including attachments and any additional information you choose to share.
  • Usage data: Log files, device identifiers, IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and referral URLs collected automatically through analytics tools.
  • Integration data: Content retrieved from connected systems or uploaded files when you enable features such as document ingestion or third-party integrations.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our products and services.
  • Respond to support requests, technical issues, and feedback.
  • Personalize user experiences, including chat interactions and knowledge base responses.
  • Monitor usage, detect fraud, and ensure the security of our infrastructure.
  • Communicate updates, marketing materials, and service announcements (only with your consent where required).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms of service.

3. Legal Bases for Processing (EEA/UK visitors)

If you are located in the European Economic Area or the United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing your personal data include:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide services you request.
  • Legitimate interests: To secure and improve our products and support user engagement.
  • Consent: For optional communications and certain integrations.
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable laws and regulations.

4. How We Share Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers: Cloud hosting, analytics, customer success, and emailing platforms bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Integrations you authorize: Third-party tools (e.g., document storage, messaging) that you connect to Workflow SG.
  • Professional advisors: Lawyers, accountants, or auditors assisting us with business operations.
  • Authorities: When required by law, subpoena, or court order, or to protect our rights and users.
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of company assets, subject to confidentiality safeguards.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, satisfy our contractual or legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. When no longer required, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

6. Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. Despite our efforts, no security system is impenetrable, so we encourage you to use strong passwords and promptly notify us of any suspected unauthorized activity.

7. International Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed on servers located in countries other than your own. When we transfer personal data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or other lawful transfer mechanisms.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections, updates, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing activities.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below. We may ask for verification before honoring a request.
